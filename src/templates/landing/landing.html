Key Background Concepts

To design a visual plume modeling tool, the team had to learn some key concepts includ the functionalities of Geographic Information System (GIS) frameworks, the syntax of JavaScript and and website development and hosting. In addition, the team developed an understanding of release rat particles, the calculation of particle concentrations and dosage using diffusion equations, and the use of statistical models in representing the physical processes of nuclide drifts.
